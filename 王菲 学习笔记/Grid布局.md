**容器属性**

1. grid-template-columns 50% 50% 或 repeat（2，50%）或1fr 1fr

3. grid-template(前面rows/后面columns)

1. grid-column-start

3. grid-row-start
4. grid-row-end
5. grid-column
6. grid-row
7. grid-area(rowstart/columnstart/rowend/columnend)
